2026 is shaping up to be an epic year for sneakerheads, with major collaborations, retros, and innovative designs hitting the shelves. From Nike's tech-infused runners to Adidas' sustainable classics, here's your must-know list of releases that could make or break your collection.

## Nike Air Force 1 '07 High 'White'
Dropping in February, this retro high-top brings back the classic silhouette with premium materials. Expect a streetwear boom as influencers flock to this timeless piece.

## Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 'Zebra'
The legendary Zebra stripes return in April with updated Boost foam for enhanced comfort. Limited stock means early raffle entries are crucial.

## Jordan 1 Retro High OG 'Chicago'
MJ's icon makes a comeback in May, featuring the original Chicago colorway. Collectors are already lining up for this heritage drop.

## New Balance 574 'Classic'
A sustainable twist on the 574 in June, with recycled materials and vibrant colors. Perfect for eco-conscious sneaker lovers.

## Puma RS-X 'Tech'
Innovative tech integrations hit in July, blending performance with style. This could be Puma's biggest release of the year.
