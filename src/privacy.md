# Privacy Policy

At **Hunting For Kicks**, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that are received and collected by our website and how that information is used.

## Routine Information Collection

Like almost all standard websites, our servers automatically track basic, non-personally identifying information about visitors. This information includes, but is not limited to, IP addresses, browser types, timestamps, and referring pages. This data is used strictly for routine website administration, analyzing traffic trends, and ensuring the site runs smoothly. None of this information can personally identify specific visitors.

## Cookies and Web Beacons

Where necessary, Hunting For Kicks uses cookies to store information about a visitor's preferences and history in order to optimize the user experience and ensure our site loads as fast as possible.

### Third-Party Links and Affiliates

As a sneaker publication, our articles may contain external links to third-party retailers, brands, or secondary markets (e.g., linking to a shoe release). We may occasionally participate in affiliate marketing programs. If you click on an external affiliate link, that third-party retailer may use cookies to track your referral from our site. 

We have no access to or control over the cookies that are used by third-party advertisers or retailers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party sites for more detailed information on their practices.

## Embedded Content from Other Websites

Articles on this site frequently include embedded content (such as images loaded from Unsplash or videos from YouTube). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if you had visited the other website directly.

These external websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content.

## Security

The security of your information is important to us. Because we operate as a static website without a traditional database or user-login system, we do not actively collect or store highly sensitive personal data (such as credit card numbers or passwords) on our servers. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Changes To This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date published and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future. We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time, and any modified terms will be updated directly on this page. 

## Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled while browsing Hunting For Kicks, please reach out to us via our official social channels.
